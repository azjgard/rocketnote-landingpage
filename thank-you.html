5 Pro Tips

1. Editing Timestamps

We've made it possible to edit timestamps by entering edit mode on a note. You'll notice a blue bar at the top of the video while in edit mode. You'll be able to scrub the video itself to change to whichever time you want the timestamp to reflect.

2. Keyboard Shortcuts

We have a lot of shortcut keys, all of which are listed on our Keyboard Shortcuts page. Give them a try, because it makes everything MUCH easier (for pro users!).

3. #feedback gives us... feedback

We aren't always listening, but submitting a note with #feedback in it will send us a notification immediately with any feedback you have.

4. Other Hashtags

We do support other hashtags as well! We haven't utilized them super well yet, but all of them are enabled and stored securely for tagging notes. More with that coming soon!
