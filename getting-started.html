A Guide To Logging In & Taking Notes

Log In

The first thing you need to do is log in. To do so:

  1. Press the red Rocket Note icon that appeared in your Chrome toolbar. Alternatively, click here to log in.
  2. Click "Log In" under the Rocket Note logo that pops up.
  3. A new tab will open, where you'll be prompted to add a Google account. We recommend choosing the account you associate with watching videos on YouTube.

That's all— you're in!

Get To It!

The Rocket Note widget will now show up next to any video you watch on YouTube. Just click the input (you'll see "Type here...") and type your note. Click the "Add" button. WE HAVE LIFT OFF. You just took your first note!
