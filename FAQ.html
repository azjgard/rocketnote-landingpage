Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download Rocket Note?

The easy way is to click "Add To Chrome" on our home page. Otherwise, feel free to search on the Chrome app store.

Do you guys have any keyboard shortcuts?

We sure do! For a full list of shortcuts, see our complete Keyboard Shortcuts page.

It's not working... Does that count as a question?

We don't just claim to have amazing customer service. Put us to the test by starting a chat (bottom-right) with a member of our support staff!

Can I use Rocket Note on my phone?

No, sorry. In the future, we'd love to somehow make this possible, but extensions don't yet exist on mobile browsers.

Is there a Rocket Note extension for Firefox?

Not yet! But we're actively working on one as we speak.
